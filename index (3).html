Laws of the Land

The King's Law (General Rules)

  • All players shall show respect to fellow members of the realm and the Small Council
  • Harassment, bullying, or treasonous speech shall not be tolerated
  • The Common Tongue (English) only in public channels
  • No spamming or flooding the raven messages
  • All must follow Roblox's Terms of Service

Rules of Engagement (Roleplay)

  • Remain in character during court sessions and battles
  • No powergaming or assuming godlike abilities
  • Respect the established lore and canon of Westeros
  • No metagaming - use only knowledge your character would possess
  • Follow the timeline and events as decreed by the Maesters

Rules for the Small Council (Staff)

  • Council members must remain active and serve faithfully
  • No abuse of royal authority (admin powers)
  • All smallfolk shall be judged fairly and equally
  • Council discussions shall remain confidential
  • Lead by example in all actions and decisions
